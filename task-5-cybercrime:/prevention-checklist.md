# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

- **Use strong, unique passwords** for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.  
- **Never share OTPs, passwords, or UPI PINs** with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank.  
- **Verify UPI collect requests carefully** – receiving money does NOT require entering your PIN.  
- **Avoid clicking on suspicious links** sent via SMS, email, or social media, especially those creating urgency.  
- **Install apps only from official stores** like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.  
- **Keep your devices updated** with the latest security patches and antivirus software.  
- **Limit personal information shared online**, including location, college ID, and phone number.  
- **Log out from shared or public computers** and avoid saving passwords on them.  
- **Use secure Wi-Fi networks** and avoid financial transactions on public Wi-Fi.  
- **Regularly monitor bank statements and UPI transactions** for any unauthorized activity.  

## 🚨 If you are a victim of cybercrime:
- Report immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** (for financial fraud reporting)
