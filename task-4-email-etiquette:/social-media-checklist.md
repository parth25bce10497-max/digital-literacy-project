# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## ✅ Do’s
- **Think before you post** – Consider how your content might be perceived by others, including future employers.  
- **Protect your privacy** – Use strong passwords and review privacy settings regularly.  
- **Be respectful and kind** – Engage in positive and constructive conversations.  
- **Verify information before sharing** – Avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.  
- **Maintain a professional image** – Remember that your online presence reflects your personal brand.  

## ❌ Don’ts
- **Don’t share personal or sensitive information** – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or financial details.  
- **Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling** – It can harm your reputation and mental well-being.  
- **Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content** – This includes hate speech, explicit material, or harmful jokes.  
- **Don’t overshare** – Posting too frequently or too much personal content can be risky.  
- **Don’t ignore digital footprints** – What you post online can stay there permanently.
